In A Magic Country Olufine and Sampia both have skill of the kirja craft, 1 uses it to imprison, the other to set free
While Samki uses his pocket-knife to carve something solid, his sister can carve things fluid and loosen the structures underneath them and so has much more control, a much closer connection. Ikka helps lots.
eg. 'She unwrapped the locks of current, caught a deep golden hue, called a hot blue fume, chased a silken green dew, flowing carried along beads of bright blooms, shivering downstream. Showing the secret fish ways forth poured the fleets of shining spawning fish, and Samki built a rod, brought his catch to the bonfire, into the clearing.'
Knotty wood, fox fur, lady beetle buttons, birch bark, river ripples, without the wood-jewel fire she wouldn't be able to know all of them as a child It's as taxing as any creative endeavour, it's a craft but it isn't magic per se
